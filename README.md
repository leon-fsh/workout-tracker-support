## Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

FSHR: Workout Tracker is an offline-first workout planning and logging app.

The app does not require an account and does not operate a backend service. Workout templates, workout logs, set entries, bodyweight entries, and app settings are stored locally on your device.

The developer does not collect, sell, or share personal data through this app.

## Data Stored on Device

FSHR: Workout Tracker may store the following information locally on your device:

- Workout templates
- Exercise names and settings
- Workout logs
- Set entries, including weight and repetitions
- Bodyweight entries
- App settings

This data remains on your device unless you choose to share it through device-level features outside the app.

## Apple Health

Apple Health integration is optional and only used if you grant permission.

If enabled, the app may read supported Apple Health data, including body weight and step count, to display it alongside your training information.

If enabled, the app may write bodyweight entries and finished workout sessions to Apple Health.

You can manage or revoke Apple Health permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app.
